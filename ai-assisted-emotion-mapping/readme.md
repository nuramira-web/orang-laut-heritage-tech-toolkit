# Heritage Emotion Mapping

Heritage Emotion Mapping helps identify emotional themes in oral histories and archive materials.

It recognises that heritage is not only factual. It is also emotional, relational, embodied, and intergenerational.

## Emotional themes may include

- belonging
- loss
- longing
- joy
- grief
- pride
- displacement
- care
- resilience
- tenderness
- nostalgia
- reverence
- continuity

## Important caution

AI may suggest emotional tags, but humans should review them.

Do not impose emotions onto speakers.

Where possible, emotional tags should be based on the speaker’s own words.
