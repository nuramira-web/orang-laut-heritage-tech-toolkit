# Disappearing Words AI Tool

The Disappearing Words AI Tool helps students, families, and community contributors document words, phrases, nicknames, sayings, ritual terms, food expressions, and maritime vocabulary that may be fading from everyday use.

The tool is designed to support intergenerational language preservation.

It helps users turn a submitted word or phrase into a draft archive entry, including:

- meaning
- context of use
- emotional tone
- possible related themes
- oral history follow-up questions
- sensitivity flags
- AI uncertainty notes

## Important principle

AI may assist with documentation, but it must not invent meaning.

All entries require human or community review before publication.
