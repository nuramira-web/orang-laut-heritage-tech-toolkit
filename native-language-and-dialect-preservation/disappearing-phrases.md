# Disappearing Phrases

This file documents phrases, sayings, expressions, nicknames, jokes, prayers, pantun, and everyday forms of speech that may no longer be widely used.

## Why this matters

Disappearing phrases preserve:

- humour
- intimacy
- family memory
- social relationships
- maritime knowledge
- ritual practice
- emotional worlds
- intergenerational voice

## Suggested categories

- greetings
- kinship expressions
- sea warnings
- food phrases
- festival phrases
- children’s games
- prayers
- pantun
- proverbs
- teasing or joking phrases
- place-based expressions
