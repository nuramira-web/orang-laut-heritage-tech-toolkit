# Pronunciation Guide

Pronunciation is an important part of oral heritage.

Where possible, vocabulary entries should include audio recordings or approximate pronunciation notes.

## Recommended fields

- term
- speaker
- language or dialect
- pronunciation note
- audio filename
- location or community connection
- permission for public use
- permission for AI use
- review status

## Recording pronunciation

When recording pronunciation:

- use a quiet room
- ask the speaker to say the word naturally
- ask for the word in a sentence
- record any explanation of tone, humour, or context
- avoid correcting dialect into standard language

## Audio file naming

Suggested format:

`PB-VOC-001_malam-tujuh-likur_pronunciation.mp3`

## Notes on variation

Different families may pronounce the same word differently.

Record variation rather than forcing one “correct” version.

## AI use

AI may help with transcription or phonetic approximation, but community review is required.
