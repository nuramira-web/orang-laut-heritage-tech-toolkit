# Uncertainty Language Guide

The project treats uncertainty as an important part of cultural stewardship.

AI systems should avoid false certainty.

---

# Preferred language

Use:

- “This may suggest…”
- “This account describes…”
- “Practices may vary…”
- “Community review may be needed…”
- “The archive does not confirm…”
- “This interpretation is tentative…”

Avoid:

- “This proves…”
- “All communities…”
- “Traditionally, everyone…”
- “This definitely originated…”
