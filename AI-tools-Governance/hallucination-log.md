# Hallucination Log

The Hallucination Log documents known AI failures, inaccuracies, distortions, and governance concerns.

The goal is not to hide AI failures.

The goal is to make them visible, reviewable, and learnable.

---

# Hallucination categories

## Invented historical claims

Examples:
- invented origins
- fabricated dates
- false migration narratives
- unsupported ritual histories

---

## Dialect mistranslation

Examples:
- flattening island Malay into standard Malay
- incorrect emotional meaning
- mistranslating maritime terms
- losing humour or intimacy

---

## Emotional flattening

Examples:
- reducing grief to nostalgia
- romanticising displacement
- removing ambiguity
- exaggerating sentimentality

---

## Cultural overgeneralisation

Examples:
- treating all Orang Laut communities as identical
- presenting one family practice as universal
- collapsing distinct ritual practices

---

## False certainty

Examples:
- presenting uncertain memory as factual
- overstating confidence
- inventing source authority
