# AI Review Checklist

Before publishing AI-assisted outputs, reviewers should ask:

## Source grounding

- Is the output supported by source material?
- Are claims cited or attributable?
- Is uncertainty preserved?

---

## Cultural nuance

- Does the output flatten communities?
- Does it preserve dialect and ritual specificity?
- Does it preserve contributor voice?

---

## Emotional integrity

- Does the AI exaggerate sentiment?
- Does it romanticise loss or displacement?
- Does it preserve emotional ambiguity?

---

## Governance

- Are permissions respected?
- Are restrictions respected?
- Does the material require community review?
- Does the output require revision?
