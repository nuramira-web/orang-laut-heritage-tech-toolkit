# Project Prompts

## 1. Build a Memory Constellation

Choose:
- one ritual
- one place
- one recipe
- one oral history

Map how they connect emotionally, spatially, historically, and socially.

---

## 2. Disappearing Words Project

Document:
- a family phrase
- a dialect word
- a maritime term
- a food expression

Record:
- pronunciation
- meaning
- context
- emotional tone
- who uses it

---

## 3. Emotional Heritage Atlas

Identify emotional themes across oral histories.

Examples:
- belonging
- loss
- pride
- care
- longing

Visualise them using:
- maps
- diagrams
- timelines
- sound
- interactive interfaces

---

## 4. Oral History Remix

Transform an interview into:
- podcast
- soundscape
- visual essay
- animation
- digital exhibit
- interactive narrative

---

## 5. AI and Heritage Audit

Test an AI tool on heritage material.

Ask:
- What did the AI misunderstand?
- What nuance was lost?
- What assumptions appeared?
- What should require human review?

---

## 6. Maritime Knowledge Explorer

Build an interface exploring:
- tides
- routes
- boats
- fishing
- weather
- shoreline change

Connect sea knowledge to memory and community life.

---

## 7. Ritual and Foodways Mapping

Choose a festival or communal ritual.

Map:
- food
- movement
- labour
- prayer
- sound
- preparation
- emotional significance

---

## 8. Build a Heritage Interface

Design a:
- chatbot
- archive explorer
- memory map
- dialect archive
- emotional timeline
- AI retrieval system

Prioritise:
- accessibility
- consent
- explainability
- cultural nuance
