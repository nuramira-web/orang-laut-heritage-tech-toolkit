# Cultural Nuance Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing AI outputs.

## Language

- Did the AI preserve the original word or phrase?
- Did it over-translate?
- Did it erase dialect?
- Did it miss humour, intimacy, or tone?

## Context

- Did the AI explain when and where the term or practice is used?
- Did it distinguish family-specific knowledge from community-wide knowledge?
- Did it avoid treating one memory as universal?

## Emotion

- Did the AI preserve emotional meaning?
- Did it impose emotions not present in the source?
- Did it recognise care, longing, grief, humour, pride, or reverence where supported?

## Sensitivity

- Did the AI expose private or sacred knowledge?
- Did it flag uncertain or sensitive material?
- Did it recommend community review where needed?

## Accuracy

- Did the AI invent details?
- Did it overstate certainty?
- Did it make historical claims without evidence?
