# Teach the AI Your Heritage

Teach the AI Your Heritage is an experimental learning tool within the Pulau Brani Project’s AI Heritage Lab.

Students attempt to teach an AI system about a piece of heritage knowledge, such as:

- a dialect word
- a family phrase
- a recipe
- a ritual
- a maritime practice
- a food memory
- an oral history fragment
- a place memory

The goal is not to produce perfect AI outputs.

The goal is to help students understand how AI systems interpret, misunderstand, simplify, or flatten cultural knowledge.

## Learning goals

Students learn to ask:

- What did the AI understand?
- What did the AI miss?
- What nuance disappeared?
- What did the AI invent?
- What should require human or community review?
- What should never be automated?

## Core principle

AI can support cultural discovery, but it cannot replace community memory, lived experience, or human stewardship.
