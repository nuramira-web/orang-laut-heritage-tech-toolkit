# Classroom Activity: Teach the AI Your Heritage

## Duration

60–90 minutes

## Suitable for

- secondary school
- junior college
- polytechnic
- university
- museum workshops
- community youth workshops

## Materials

- one heritage word, phrase, recipe, ritual, or memory
- teaching template
- AI tool
- response analysis sheet
- cultural nuance checklist

## Activity flow

### Step 1: Choose a heritage item

Students choose something to teach the AI.

Examples:

- a family phrase
- a food memory
- a ritual
- a dialect word
- a place memory
- a sea term

### Step 2: Fill out the teaching template

Students document:

- meaning
- context
- emotional tone
- sensitivity
- possible misunderstandings

### Step 3: Run the AI tool

Students paste their material into the AI tool.

### Step 4: Analyse the response

Students identify:

- accuracy
- hallucination
- flattening
- missing nuance
- sensitivity issues

### Step 5: Improve the prompt

Students rewrite the prompt and test again.

### Step 6: Reflect

Students discuss:

- What can AI help with?
- What should humans review?
- What should communities govern?
