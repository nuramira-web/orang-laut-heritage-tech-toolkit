# Hallucination Detection Guide

AI hallucination happens when an AI system produces information that sounds confident but is not supported by the source material.

## Watch for invented details

The AI may invent:

- dates
- names
- places
- rituals
- origins
- translations
- family relationships
- historical explanations
- religious meanings

## Warning signs

Be careful when the AI says:

- “traditionally, this always means…”
- “this proves that…”
- “all Orang Laut communities…”
- “this ritual originated from…”
- “this was practiced by everyone…”

## Safer wording

Prefer:

- “In this account…”
- “This family remembers…”
- “This may suggest…”
- “More community review is needed…”
- “The source material does not confirm…”
