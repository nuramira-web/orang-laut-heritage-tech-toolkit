# Classroom Activity: AI Remix Studio

## Duration

90–120 minutes

## Suitable for

- secondary school
- junior college
- polytechnic
- university
- museum workshops
- youth heritage labs

## Learning goals

Students will:

- create an AI-assisted heritage remix
- evaluate AI’s creative role
- identify cultural nuance and ethical risks
- practise source-grounded storytelling
- reflect on stewardship in the AI era

## Activity flow

### Step 1: Choose source material

Students select one approved archive item, oral history excerpt, vocabulary entry, ritual memory, or recipe.

### Step 2: Choose remix format

Examples:

- podcast
- short video
- soundscape
- visual essay
- digital zine
- memory collage

### Step 3: Generate AI draft

Students use the AI Remix Studio tool to create a draft.

### Step 4: Human revision

Students revise for:

- accuracy
- tone
- context
- dignity
- cultural specificity
- uncertainty

### Step 5: Self-critique

Students complete the self-critique framework.

### Step 6: Share and discuss

Students present:

- the remix
- what AI helped with
- what AI misunderstood
- what they changed
- whether it should be public
