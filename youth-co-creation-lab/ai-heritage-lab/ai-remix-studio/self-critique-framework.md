# Self-Critique Framework

Every AI-assisted remix should include a short self-critique.

## 1. Source grounding

- What source material did you use?
- What permissions were attached to it?
- What did you exclude?

## 2. Creative transformation

- What did you change?
- What form did you choose?
- Why did you choose this form?

## 3. AI contribution

- What did AI help with?
- What did AI suggest?
- What did you reject?
- What did you revise?

## 4. Cultural evaluation

- What nuance was preserved?
- What nuance was lost?
- Did AI flatten dialect, emotion, ritual, or place?
- Did the remix overgeneralise?

## 5. Ethical evaluation

- Is anything sensitive?
- Does this require community review?
- Is the contributor treated with dignity?
- Is the remix suitable for public sharing?

## 6. Final reflection

Complete this sentence:

“This remix helped me understand that cultural stewardship in the AI era requires…”
