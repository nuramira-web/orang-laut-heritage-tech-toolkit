# Remix Principles

## 1. Remix with care

Heritage materials are not neutral content. They may carry memory, grief, family relationships, ritual meaning, displacement, faith, humour, and belonging.

## 2. Preserve source dignity

Do not use archive materials in ways that ridicule, sensationalise, decontextualise, or trivialise contributors.

## 3. Preserve context

A remix should not separate a story from its place, community, language, ritual, or emotional meaning.

## 4. Consent matters

Only remix materials approved for public, educational, or creative use.

## 5. AI is a collaborator, not an authority

AI can suggest forms, scripts, captions, structures, and interpretations. Humans must review the output.

## 6. Self-critique is part of the work

Every AI-assisted remix should include a reflection on what the AI changed, misunderstood, omitted, or made possible.

## 7. Creativity should deepen stewardship

The goal is not content production. The goal is more thoughtful participation in cultural memory.
