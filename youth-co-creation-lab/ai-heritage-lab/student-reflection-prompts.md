# Student Reflection Prompts

- What surprised you about oral history?
- What kinds of knowledge are difficult for AI systems to interpret?
- What did the AI misunderstand?
- What responsibilities come with preserving memory?
- What stories are missing from official history?
- How does emotion shape heritage?
- What should remain private or community-restricted?
- What does ethical stewardship mean?
- What would responsible AI look like in your own community?
- How should younger generations participate in preserving memory?
