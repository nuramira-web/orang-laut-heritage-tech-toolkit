# Community Brief Generator

The Community Brief Generator helps turn archive findings, oral histories, workshop outputs, and youth reflections into short, accessible public briefs.

These briefs may support:

- community updates
- school resources
- heritage awareness
- policy conversations
- grant proposals
- museum summaries
- public education
- workshop documentation

---

# Core principle

A good community brief should:

- be accessible
- preserve nuance
- remain evidence-grounded
- respect contributor dignity
- explain public relevance clearly
