# Storytelling Risks

Public storytelling can unintentionally distort heritage.

## Risks include

- flattening complexity
- sensationalism
- over-romanticisation
- “vanishing culture” narratives
- historical overclaiming
- treating communities as static
- turning grief into content
- speaking for communities without review
- aestheticising displacement

## AI-specific risks

AI systems may:

- exaggerate emotional language
- overgeneralise
- invent historical certainty
- simplify dialect and ritual
- reproduce stereotypes
- prioritise dramatic narrative structure over nuance

## Safer practices

- preserve uncertainty
- cite sources
- invite review
- include community voices
- explain context
- avoid universal claims
