# Narrative Builder

The Narrative Builder helps students, educators, community members, and heritage practitioners turn memories, archive materials, oral histories, community concerns, and public-interest issues into clear and ethical public narratives.

The goal is not marketing or manipulation.

The goal is thoughtful public storytelling grounded in:

- evidence
- memory
- emotion
- historical context
- audience awareness
- community dignity

---

# What the tool helps create

The Narrative Builder may help generate:

- presentation outlines
- public storytelling arcs
- exhibition narratives
- short advocacy statements
- workshop framing
- school project narratives
- community messages
- heritage awareness campaigns
- public engagement material

---

# Core questions

The tool encourages users to ask:

- Why does this matter?
- Who is affected?
- What emotional truths are present?
- What evidence supports this?
- What is at stake?
- What future is being imagined?
- What should remain nuanced or uncertain?
- What should require community review?

---

# Important principle

A strong narrative should clarify complexity, not erase it.
