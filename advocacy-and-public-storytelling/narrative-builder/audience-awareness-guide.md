# Audience Awareness Guide

Different audiences require different framing.

The same issue may need to be explained differently to:

- students
- teachers
- policymakers
- museums
- funders
- community members
- journalists
- researchers

## Questions to ask

- What does this audience already know?
- What assumptions might they have?
- What language is accessible?
- What emotional framing is appropriate?
- What level of detail is needed?
- What should remain nuanced?
- What action or reflection is being invited?

## Important

Adapting for audience should not mean distorting community meaning.
