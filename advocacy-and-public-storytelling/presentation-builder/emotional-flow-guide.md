# Emotional Flow Guide

Presentations shape emotional experience.

Good emotional pacing helps audiences stay engaged without manipulation.

## Productive emotional flows

- curiosity
- recognition
- reflection
- connection
- responsibility
- participation
- hope

## Risky emotional flows

- trauma spectacle
- pity
- exaggerated urgency
- nostalgia without complexity
- emotional overload
- saviour framing

## Questions to ask

- What emotional experience is being created?
- Is the emotional framing supported by evidence?
- Does it preserve dignity?
- Does it leave space for reflection?
